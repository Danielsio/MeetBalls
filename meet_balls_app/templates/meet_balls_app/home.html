Welcome to MeetBalls

MeetBalls is an app that lets you schedule events to play with people near you. Whether you're into sports, games, or just want to hang out, MeetBalls makes it easy to find like-minded people to play with.

With MeetBalls, you can:

  • Schedule an event for a specific activity, location, date, and time
  • Invite others to join your event and specify how many extra people you want to play with
  • Chat with the other users in a group chat to coordinate and plan your event
  • Browse and join other events that have been scheduled by other users
